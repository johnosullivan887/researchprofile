John O’Sullivan

Postdoctoral Researcher, Department of Materials, University of Oxford

I am an experimental researcher working on transparent conducting electrodes, semiconductor interfaces, and tandem photovoltaic devices. My research focuses on thin-film materials, charge transport, optical and electrical characterisation, and scalable device integration for next-generation solar technologies.

I am currently based in the Department of Materials at Oxford, where my work combines semiconductor thin-film fabrication, interface engineering, and device characterisation. I have also contributed to undergraduate teaching, laboratory demonstrating, and student mentoring.
