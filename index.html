Experimental research in semiconductor materials and interfaces.

I am a physicist and materials researcher working on semiconductor thin films, transparent conductors, and device interfaces for optoelectronic applications. My research combines fabrication, electrical and optical characterisation, spectroscopy, and device modelling to understand how material properties shape performance.

Research

Transparent conductors

Graphene, ZnO, AZO, and related thin films for optoelectronic and energy applications.

Interfaces and transport

Energy alignment, contact resistance, interface passivation, and charge extraction.

Characterisation

Hall effect, Van der Pauw, UV-Vis, Kelvin probe, X-ray absorption spectroscopy, Raman, and device testing.

Modelling

Device-level simulations to connect measured material properties to performance limits.
